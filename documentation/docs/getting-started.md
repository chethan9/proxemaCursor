# Get started

## Before you begin

- You need an account invitation or signup flow from your organization.
- Your admin assigns **roles** and **permissions**—some areas (for example **Clients**, parts of **Developer**, or **Administration**) may be hidden unless your role allows them.

## Sign in and orientation

1. Open your Proxima Cursor URL (provided by your team).
2. Sign in with your credentials.
3. Use the **primary sidebar** to move between areas such as **Health** (dashboard), **Projects**, **Templates**, **Billing**, and **Settings**.

:::tip Projects vs store workspace

In the product UI, **Projects** holds your connected stores. Selecting a project opens the **store workspace** where **Home**, **Orders**, **Products**, and related tools live—each under a path like `/sites/[site]/…`.

:::

## Connect your first store (typical flow)

Exact steps depend on how your organization provisions WooCommerce credentials and store records. In general:

1. Go to **Projects** and open or create the store entry your admin configured.
2. Complete any **connection** or **preferences** onboarding your team uses (credentials, URL, sync behavior).
3. Wait for an initial **sync** or follow your team’s checklist before editing catalog data.

If something blocks onboarding (permissions, missing credentials, or a stuck sync), see [Troubleshooting](./troubleshooting).

## Recommended first tasks

| Goal | Where to go |
|------|-------------|
| See store health / activity | **Health** (`/`) |
| Open a store | **Projects** → choose a site |
| Review orders | Store workspace → **Orders** |
| Review products | Store workspace → **Products** |
| Check sync status | **Sync Runs** (if visible for your role) |
