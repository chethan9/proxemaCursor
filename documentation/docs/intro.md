# Proxima Cursor — Help Center

**Proxima Cursor** connects your WooCommerce stores to a unified workspace so your team can manage products, orders, customers, and catalog data from one place—with sync, automation hooks, and optional AI-assisted workflows.

These guides are written for **people using the product day to day** (not developers integrating APIs).

### What you can do here

- Open **Projects** and jump into a connected **store workspace**
- Review **Home** metrics and recent activity for a store
- Manage **Products**, **Orders**, **Customers**, **Categories**, **Attributes**, **Tags**, **Brands**, **Downloads**, and **Bulk Jobs**
- Track **Sync runs**, **Webhooks**, and **API** access where your role allows
- Handle **Billing**, **Plans**, **Payment methods**, and **Referrals**
- Manage **Users**, **Roles**, and **profile settings**

Where exact labels depend on your organization’s menu customization, use this Help Center together with what you see in the sidebar.
