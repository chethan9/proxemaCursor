# Projects & Templates

## Projects

**Projects** (`/projects`) is where you choose which connected store workspace to open. Each row represents a site integrated with your Proxima Cursor tenant.

**Typical workflow**

1. Open **Projects**.
2. Select a store to enter its workspace (Home, Orders, Products, etc.).
3. Use **Configuration** under the store menu when you need store-specific preferences allowed by your role.

## Templates

**Templates** (`/templates`) supports reusable definitions your organization authors for repeatable setups—follow internal naming conventions when selecting which template applies.
