# Users, roles & profile settings

## Organization directory

- **Users** — invite colleagues, adjust membership, and deactivate accounts according to policy.
- **Roles** — maps permissions (such as catalog edits vs billing visibility). Request changes through your administrator when you need broader access.

## Personal settings

**Settings → Profile** hosts individual preferences (locale, profile fields, security choices exposed by your deployment).

## Administration (restricted)

Sections such as **Billing Controls**, **Payment Logs**, **Referrals (admin)**, **Platform Tasks**, **Cloudflare Images**, **AI features**, **AI providers**, and **Languages** are aimed at operators—if you do not see them, your role does not include administrative scopes.
