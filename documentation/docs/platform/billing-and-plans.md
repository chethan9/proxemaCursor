# Billing, plans & referrals

## Billing overview

**Billing** summarizes subscription status, invoices, and account-level payment posture where Stripe (or your configured gateway) is integrated.

## Payment methods

**Payment Methods** stores cards or other instruments permitted by your gateway configuration.

## Plans

**Plans** (`/pricing`) explains tiers available to your organization—exact SKUs and limits are defined by your admins.

## Referrals

**Referrals** appears when your tenant participates in referral incentives—eligibility and payouts follow program rules defined by your organization.

:::note Accounts vs organization billing

Some enterprises invoice centrally; others allow self-serve card billing. Follow your finance owner’s guidance before changing payment methods.

:::
