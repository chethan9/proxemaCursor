# Home dashboard

Each connected store has a **Home** workspace (`/sites/[site]/home`) with cards and charts that summarize recent activity—such as orders and product performance—so you can orient quickly without drilling into every list page.

## What you’ll use it for

- Spot trends or anomalies before opening detailed lists
- Jump to common follow-ups (for example recent orders or popular products—depending on what your organization surfaces)

## Tips

- Currency or locale switches may appear when your store supports multiple contexts—pick the one your team standardizes on for reporting.
- If numbers look stale, your instance may refresh summaries on a schedule; wait a few minutes or ask an admin whether manual refresh is available.
