# Categories, tags, brands & attributes

Taxonomy keeps large catalogs searchable and powers variable products.

## Categories & navigation

Use **Categories** to maintain hierarchical category trees aligned with how shoppers browse your store.

## Tags

**Tags** provide flexible labeling across products—useful for campaigns and featured collections when your merchandising team agrees on a tagging scheme.

## Brands

**Brands** groups products by manufacturer or label when your catalog uses brand-led discovery.

## Attributes

**Attributes** define variation axes (size, color) and may include attribute terms. Changes here can affect variation matrices—coordinate with merchandising before renaming core attributes.

:::caution

Bulk taxonomy edits can impact SEO URLs and variation combinations in WooCommerce. Prefer staged updates when possible.

:::
