# Bulk jobs & downloads

## Bulk Jobs

**Bulk Jobs** tracks longer-running batch operations—such as bulk imports or exports your organization uses for catalog maintenance. Status, artifacts, and retention policies depend on admin configuration.

**If a job appears stuck**

- Refresh the list after a few minutes.
- Ask an admin to verify worker/cron health—operations teams often reconcile stuck jobs from platform tasks.

## Downloads

**Downloads** manages downloadable items linked to products when your WooCommerce catalog includes digital goods.
