# Orders & customers

## Orders

Open **Orders** from the store workspace to review WooCommerce orders associated with the site. Typical actions include opening an order record to verify totals, status, line items, and addresses—exact capabilities depend on your role.

**Tips**

- Use filters or date ranges when working across busy seasons.
- If an order looks out of date, confirm whether sync jobs completed successfully ([Sync & automation](../platform/sync-webhooks-developer)).

## Customers

**Customers** lists people who have placed orders or otherwise appear in WooCommerce customer records for the site.

**Tips**

- Customer creation or edits may be constrained by WooCommerce rules—follow your team’s data governance practices.
- When profile fields fail validation (email format, required shipping fields), fix the underlying values before retrying.
