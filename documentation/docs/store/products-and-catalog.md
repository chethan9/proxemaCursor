# Products & catalog editing

From the store workspace, open **Products** to browse, create, and edit WooCommerce catalog items. Product editing supports structured tabs—such as core details, variations, pricing and tax, inventory and shipping, and media—so complex catalogs stay organized.

## Create or edit a product

1. Go to **Products**.
2. Choose **New product** or open an existing row.
3. Fill in **basic information** (title, descriptions, status, and catalog placement).
4. Configure **variations** when your catalog uses variable products—attributes drive SKUs and pricing at the variation level.
5. Save changes. Sync timing depends on your organization’s workflows (immediate API writes vs batched jobs).

## AI-assisted workflows (if enabled)

Some environments expose AI tooling for images or content assistance. Availability depends on **organization settings** and **feature flags** configured by administrators.

## Related areas

- **Categories**, **Tags**, **Brands**, and **Attributes**—taxonomy that powers navigation, filters, and variation logic
- **Downloads**—downloadable assets attached to products where applicable
